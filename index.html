Mesa/Boogie Brutality

The Mesa/Boogie JP-2C isn't just a collection of words, the JP-2C represents over 30 years of high gain amplifier development wrapped in an awesome package. Since Mesa/Boogie started they have NEVER built a custom amplifier for anyone. Until John Petrucci (That handsome devil with the beard up and to the left, click his face for more fun) came along with more guitar chops than you can shake...a shaker at... Anyway. If you've ever felt like you needed more gain, or just want to be able to go from high gain, to crystal clear and have more features than you can comprehend; GET THE JP-2C. Also JP-2C stands for John Petrucci Mesa Mark IIc+ - It's based on his personal Mesa Mark IIc+ amplifier, but with way more features and gain.
